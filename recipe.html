Revolver StandOff

Description

A tequila cocktail that'll make the night feel like the western stand off. Spiced up with cinnamon that's prefect for fall season finishing with cane sugar syrup and bitters for that duo bitter-sweet duel.

Ingredients

  • 1.5oz Cabeza tequila
  • 1 oz Cocchi Vermouth di Torino
  • .25 oz Giffard Pamplemousse
  • 0.5 oz Sri Lankan Cinnamon-Infused Cabeza tequila
  • 1 tsp cane syrup
  • 1 dash mole bitters

Directions

  1. Take the both tequilas, Vermouth, and Pamplemousse and place in shaker with ice
  2. Shake Ingredients until frosty
  3. In a highball glass, add cane syrup and mole bitters at the bottom
  4. Pour shaken liqour into the highball glass
  5. Garnish with a twine holding a cinnamon that is lit on one end, around the glass
