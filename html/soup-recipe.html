French Onion Soup

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 4 medium onions, sliced
  • 2 chopped garlic cloves
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 thyme sprigs
  • Pinch of kosher salt and black pepper
  • 1 cup of Cabernet Sauvignon red wine
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 quarts beef broth
  • 1 baguette sliced ince thick
  • 1/2 pound grated Gruyere cheese

Directions

  1. Melt the butter in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add the onions, garlic, bay leaves, thyme, and salt and pepper to pot.
  3. Cook until the onions are caramelized (25 minutes).
  4. Add the wine, bring to a boil, reduce the heat.
  5. Simmer until the wine has evaporated and the onions are dry. (5 minutes).
  6. Remove bay leaves and thyme sprigs.
  7. Add flour to the onions and give them a stir.
  8. Turn the heat to medium low and cook the flour (10 minutes).
  9. Add the beef broth and bring the soup back to a simmer (10 minutes).
  10. Add pinch of salt and pepper to your liking.
  11. Preheat the broiler. Place baguette slices on a baking sheet.
  12. Add Gruyere on top of bread and broil until bubbly and golden brown.
  13. Place soup in bowls and add slices of the Gruyere baguette on top.
